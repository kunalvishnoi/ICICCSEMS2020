Steps For Registration (Author)

  1. Revise your paper according to the review comments and minimize the similarity (refer to the notification mail for review comments).
  2. Prepare the CRC (Camera Ready Copy) as per submission template. Click Here
  3. Name the CRC paper ICCSEMS2020_idnumber.docx and ICCSEMS2020_idnumber.pdf (idnumber is the paper id given by EasyChair at the time of paper submission)
    Example: ICCSEMS2020_ id01.docx and ICCSEMS2020_ id01.pdf
  4. Download and Complete the Copyright Form Click Here Fill it, sign it and scan this filled form. Format for naming of signed and scanned copy of the copy right form is CRICCSEMS2020_ idnumber.pdf
    Example: CRICCSEMS2020_ id01.pdf
  5. Incorporate the ICCSEMS2020_idnumber.docx and ICCSEMS2020_idnumber.pdf and CR ICCSEMS2020_ idnumber.pdf in a folder name ICCSEMS2020-idnumber
  6. Download and complete the Registration Form Click Here
  7. Finish the payment.
  8. Send the following in a zipped file to us at internationalconference@jssaten.ac.in latest by 7 th March 2020.
    1. Your final papers and copyright form in a folder as described above.
    2. Filled registration form
    3. Copy of payment receipt
    4. Scanned copy of ID and bonafide certificate from the Head of the Institution/Department, if you are availing student discount
    5. IEEE/ CSI membership card, if you are availing IEEE/CSI membership discount
