Mr. Krishnamoorthy

Ex. Vice President Global Head Smart Buildings L & T

Krishnamoorthy has over 35 years of industrial experience in developing new products, innovative technology and conceptualised many new designs in the area of Industrial Products. He did his B.Tech degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Calicut and Masterâ€™s degree in Applied Electronics from PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore. He started his career with an Indian Engineering Conglomerate in Mysore, India for setting up and R&D centre in the area of Factory Automation and Robotics and worked over 13 years in developing various products for shop floor automation in manufacturing. Subsequent to that he ventured into a start-up in the area of Factory and Industrial Automation for more than 7 years and was providing Motion control Solutions for Asia Pacific in a partnership with a well known Motion Control Company out of USA.

He joined an Indian Multinational Engineering Conglomerate in their Engineering Services Vertical and incubated Motion Control and Power Electronic Practice and setup several labs in the area of Motion Control, Lighting, Building Automation and Robotics. He retired from their services after serving around 15 years and was instrumental in creating innovative Smart Building Platform. He has won Several National Awards for Best Product Designs and have patents in the area of Power Electronics and Building Automation. He is also an avid practitioner of Spiritual teachings and conducted several value-based leadership programs for young engineers and first-time managers to become successful leaders in their area of work.

Dr Saeed Sharif

Universiti of East London

Dr Saeed Sharif received his PhD in Artificial Intelligence and Medical Image Analysis from Brunel University London. He is a member of the IET and has a prestigious CEng professional status. He is a regular reviewer for a number of national and international journals and conferences such as IET Image Processing Journal, Elsevier Computers in Biology and Medicine Journal, EURASIP Journal on Image and Video Processing, IEEE Biomedical Circuits and Systems conference (BioCAS), IEEE International Symposium on Consumer Electronics (ISCE), International Conference on Information Sciences, Signal Processing and their Applications, UK Workshop on Computational Intelligence. He is a member of the technical committee of a number of international conferences such as IEEE International Conference on Computer Systems and Applications and IEEE International Conference on Computer and Information Technology. He received many academic and research awards such as ResCon2012 and Academic Mastery Certificates.
