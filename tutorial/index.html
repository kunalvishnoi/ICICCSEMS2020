Secret key generation mechanisms for resource constrained devices.

Internet-of-things(IoT) ecosystem has enabled seamless connectivity for each and every devices around us, that are capable of connecting to internet by either wired and wireless mechanisms. Also, the advancement of technologies have lead to the emergence of miniaturized personal devices like smart wearable devices, implanted medical devices and wireless sensors for various applications. Since these devices handle sensitive information, and also the fact that wireless medium is vulnerable to many types of active and passive attacks, strong security mechanisms must be employed to protect the data exchanged during communication. However, traditional security mechanisms used in high end devices/systems may not be suitable for these small devices due to resource constraints. In this tutorial, we discuss about the lightweight and Robust secret key generation mechanisms for such resource constrained devices. We also discuss about possible future research directions.


Cloud Computing Essentials

Cloud computing is a term referred to storing and accessing data over the internet. It doesn't store any data on the hard disk of your personal computer. In cloud computing, you can access data from a remote server. Bringing the business on clouds is now becoming a very common strategy for most of the companies because of enormous benefits clouds offers which includes security, elasticity, scalability, reliability and much more. Talk about any business, cloud computing finds good applications there. It could potentially lower expenses on hardware and IT. Also, it makes upgrades flawless.
